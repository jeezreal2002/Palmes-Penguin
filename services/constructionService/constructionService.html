Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our construction services

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and size. Residential projects typically take 6-12 months, while commercial projects may take 12-24 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project requirements.

Do you handle all necessary permits and approvals?

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process, including building permits, environmental clearances, and local approvals. Our experienced team works with relevant authorities to ensure all legal requirements are met before construction begins.

What payment terms do you offer?

We typically work with a milestone-based payment schedule. This includes an initial deposit (20-30%), followed by payments at key project milestones. We can customize payment terms based on project size and client preferences, including options for bank financing.

Can you work with my own architectural designs?

Absolutely! We're happy to work with your existing architectural plans or collaborate with your chosen architect. We can also provide design-build services if you prefer to have a single point of responsibility for both design and construction.

What warranties do you provide for your construction work?

We offer warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the package you choose. These warranties cover structural elements, workmanship, and materials. Additionally, manufacturer warranties for fixtures and equipment are passed directly to you.

How do you ensure quality control during construction?

We implement a rigorous quality control system that includes regular inspections by project managers, third-party quality assessments, material testing, and detailed documentation. We also welcome client site visits and provide progress reports throughout construction.
