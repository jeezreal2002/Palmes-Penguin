Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bridal car services

How far in advance should I book the bridal car?

We recommend booking your bridal car at least 6 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season (November to February). This ensures availability of your preferred vehicle.

What is included in the car decoration?

Our basic decoration includes ribbons and a "Just Married" sign. Premium decorations include fresh floral arrangements for the hood and interior. We can also customize decorations to match your wedding theme.

What happens if the car breaks down on our wedding day?

All our vehicles undergo thorough maintenance checks before each event. In the unlikely event of a breakdown, we always have backup vehicles on standby to ensure there's no disruption to your special day.

Can we choose a specific car model?

Yes, you can select specific car models from our fleet, subject to availability. We recommend visiting our showroom to see the vehicles in person before making your final decision.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made 90 days or more before the wedding date receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations between 30-90 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 30 days of the wedding date are non-refundable.

Do you offer service outside of Metro Manila?

Yes, we offer our services throughout Luzon. Additional fees may apply depending on the distance. Please contact us for a custom quote for locations outside Metro Manila.
