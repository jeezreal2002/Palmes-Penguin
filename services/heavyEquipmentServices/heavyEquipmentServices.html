Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our heavy equipment services

What types of heavy equipment do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of heavy equipment including excavators, bulldozers, wheel loaders, backhoe loaders, road rollers, dump trucks, cranes, and more. Contact us with your specific requirements.

Do you provide operators with the equipment?

Yes, we offer both equipment-only rentals and rentals with certified operators. Our monthly packages include professional operators, while daily and weekly rentals can be upgraded to include operators for an additional fee.

What is your delivery radius?

We typically deliver equipment within a 100km radius of Metro Manila. For projects beyond this area, additional transportation fees may apply. We can service most locations throughout Luzon and can arrange equipment for projects in Visayas and Mindanao.

What is included in the rental price?

Our standard rental prices include the equipment, delivery and pickup, basic fuel allowance, equipment insurance, and technical support. Operator services, extended fuel allowances, and additional attachments are available at additional costs.

How far in advance should I book equipment?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for most equipment. For larger projects requiring multiple machines or specialized equipment, 3-4 weeks notice is preferred to ensure availability.

What happens if equipment breaks down during my rental?

In the rare event of equipment breakdown due to mechanical issues, we will promptly repair or replace the equipment at no additional cost. Our technical team is available 24/7 to address any issues that may arise during your rental period.
