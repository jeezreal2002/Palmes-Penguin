Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our towing services

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?

Our average response time is 20-30 minutes in metropolitan areas. For remote locations, it may take longer, but we'll always provide you with an accurate ETA when you call.

Do you tow motorcycles?

Yes, we provide specialized motorcycle towing services using flatbed trucks equipped with proper securing equipment to ensure your motorcycle is transported safely without damage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment options like GCash and PayMaya. For corporate clients, we also offer invoicing and account-based payment solutions.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers in the Philippines. We can help process your insurance claim and, in many cases, bill your insurance directly depending on your coverage.

How far can you tow my vehicle?

We can tow your vehicle virtually any distance required. Local towing within the city is our most common service, but we also provide long-distance towing between cities and provinces for an additional fee.

Can you tow my car if the keys are locked inside?

Yes, our service vehicles are equipped with specialized tools that allow us to safely tow vehicles with locked steering wheels or transmission locks. We can also provide lockout services to help you retrieve keys locked in your vehicle.
